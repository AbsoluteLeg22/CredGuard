Accessibility

CredGuard aims to provide an accessible and inclusive experience for all users.


Our Accessibility Approach

CredGuard has been designed to support clear navigation, readable content and interactive feedback for users exploring password security concepts.

  • Responsive layouts for desktop and mobile devices
  • Clear headings and page structure
  • Readable text with consistent spacing
  • Keyboard-accessible buttons and controls
  • Password feedback presented in both text and visual form

Features Included

CredGuard includes a number of features intended to improve accessibility and ease of use.

  • Theme toggle for light and dark mode
  • Live password guidance and suggestions
  • Simple navigation across all major pages
  • Consistent button styling and page layout
  • Text labels alongside visual indicators where possible

Known Limitations

As an educational prototype, CredGuard may still have areas that could be improved in future versions.

  • Some animated elements may be distracting for certain users
  • Colour contrast may be improved further in future revisions
  • Advanced screen reader testing is limited in this prototype version

Future Improvements

Planned accessibility improvements may include reduced-motion options, enhanced keyboard navigation, stronger ARIA support and wider assistive technology testing.
